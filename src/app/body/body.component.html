The ADR Balears is an instrumental entity of the Ministry of Enterprise, Employment and Energy of the Government of the Balearic Islands. The Regional Development Agency of the Balearic Islands aims to promote the development of economic and business activity in the Balearic Islands, with criteria of competitiveness, sustainability, territorial and sectoral rebalancing, making available to entrepreneurs and businesses, especially micro, small and medium enterprises, the information and resources necessary for their implementation and to improve their management and increase their competitiveness, either directly or through coordination and collaboration with other public and private entities that pursue similar objectives.
